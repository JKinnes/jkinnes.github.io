Mission Statement

Our mission is to protect the world’s oceans by reducing the commonality of trawl fishing as a dominant source of caught fish and by tightening legislation regarding the practice. We aim to inspire new college students studying ocean engineering and related fields to make changes and impacts to this practive while they progress through their careers.

Trawling at a Glance

Fishing boat carrying a trawl net in its wake (Mudalige)

Trawling is an incredibly popular fishing method that has both massive benefits as well as massive detriments. While trawling is an undoubtedly useful fishing method, we aim to see regulation and accountability turn it from an increasingly unsustainable practice to one that can be safely and humanely performed.
