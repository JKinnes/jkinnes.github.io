Joshua Kinnes is an Engineering Technician IV employed by MIKEL Inc. supporting test and evaluation efforts for the NUWC Newport Trident LCSF.
He worked previously as a Logistician for MIKEL insupport of the NAVSUP CPGH depot and spent 6 years serving as a 15T UH-60 Black Hawk Helicopter maintainer and crew chief in the Rhode Island Army National Guard.
He deployed to Iraq and Kuwait in 2017 in support of Operation Inherent Resolve / Operation Spartan Shield, gaining hundreds of hours in maintenance experience across multiple Army aviation platforms.
His time since separation from the Army has been spent gaining experience in the test, evaluation, and maintenance of Ohio class submarine combat systems
